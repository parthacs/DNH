New Homeowner Tips and Tricks

Congratulations on your new home! Here are some essential tips to help you settle in and maintain your investment.

Initial Tasks

  • Change the locks - You don't know who has keys to your home
  • Locate the main water shut-off valve - Essential knowledge for preventing water damage
  • Locate the circuit breaker box - Know which switch controls what area
  • Replace HVAC filters - Start with fresh filters for better air quality
  • Check smoke and carbon monoxide detectors - Test and replace batteries if needed
